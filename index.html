About Us

LogiQ MFG Services Pvt. Ltd. is a Hyderabad-based Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) company committed to delivering high-quality manufacturing solutions for a wide range of electronic products. Located in Moosapet, Hyderabad, our facility provides reliable and efficient manufacturing support to businesses across various industries.

Our expertise includes PCB Assembly, SMT Assembly, Through-Hole Assembly, Product Integration, Box Build Assembly, Testing, Inspection, and Design Services. We focus on quality, precision, and customer satisfaction while ensuring on-time project delivery.

With a dedicated team and modern manufacturing practices, LogiQ MFG Services aims to be a trusted partner for companies seeking dependable electronics manufacturing solutions.
